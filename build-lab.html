Build Lab

Build Lab is the focused workspace inside DublinHacks where teams build, test, and demo with real-time mentor support, production-ready tooling, and sponsor resources. It is designed to help students ship confidently within a single day.

Reserve a Spot Partner with Build Lab

What happens in Build Lab

The lab blends focused work time with structured support so teams can stay on track, get unstuck quickly, and deliver clear demos by the end of the day.

Guided build cycles

Mentor check-ins, milestone prompts, and demo prep keep momentum high without interrupting flow.

Product-ready tooling

Design kits, cloud credits, and deployment support are available on-site so teams can ship with confidence.

Collaborative studio energy

Teams work side-by-side with access to mentors, workshops, and live feedback throughout the day.

Build Lab flow

Our schedule balances uninterrupted building time with key touchpoints that help teams stay aligned.

Morning

Kickoff + team alignment

Define project scope, select tracks, and confirm build milestones with mentors.

Midday

Mentor office hours

Get quick technical reviews, design feedback, and strategy adjustments.

Afternoon

Demo readiness

Polish features, rehearse storytelling, and prepare a confident final demo.

Support designed for students

Build Lab prioritizes clarity, access, and support so every student can contribute meaningful work.

Mentorship desk

Dedicated mentors help with architecture choices, debugging, and pitching.

Workshop boosts

Quick skill sessions unlock modern web, AI, and product workflows on demand.

Well-supported teams

Team matching, pacing guidance, and checklists keep projects on track.
