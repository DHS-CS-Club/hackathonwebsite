Mission

DublinHacks empowers students across the Bay Area to explore technology, build confidence, and ship meaningful projects in a supportive environment.

Who Should Join

Beginners and experienced builders alike. We provide workshops, mentors, and teammates so you can focus on creating.

What You'll Build

Apps, hardware prototypes, AI tools, and more. Pitch your idea, get feedback, and demo to judges at the end.
