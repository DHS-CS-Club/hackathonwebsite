FAQ

Questions? We have answers to help you prepare.

Do I need experience?

No. We welcome beginners and provide workshops to help you ship confidently.

How do teams work?

Teams can be 1–4 people. We help match solo hackers with teammates on-site.

What should I bring?

Laptop, charger, and a willingness to learn. We provide food, Wi-Fi, and mentors.

How are projects judged?

Judges score creativity, impact, and technical execution. Every team gets feedback.
